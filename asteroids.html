Shoot the Asteroids!

Catch the stars!
Shoot the asteroids!
If an asteroid touches you... game over.

CONTROLS

Left Arrow: Move Left
Right Arrow: Move Right
Space Bar: Fire

Scoring

Catch a star: +5 pts
Shoot a star: -3 pts
Let a star go by: -1 pts

Catch a special star: +10 pts
Shoot a special star: -7 pts
Let a special star go by: -5 pts

Catch an asteroid: Game Over!
Shoot an asteroid: +5 pts
Let an asteroid go by: -3 pts
